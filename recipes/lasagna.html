Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
    • Ricotta
    • Mozzarella
    • Parmesan
  • Ground Beef
  • Garlic
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Olive Oil
  • Fresh Basil

Steps

  1. Boil noodles for 8 minutes. Drain.
  2. Put a layer of sauce on bottom of baking dish.
  3. Add a layer of noodles and cheese.
  4. Repeat until out of sauce and noodles.
  5. Top with cheese.
  6. Bake at 400 degrees for one hour. Remove. Let cool. Enjoy!
