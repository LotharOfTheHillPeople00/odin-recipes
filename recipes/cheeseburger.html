Ingredients

  • Cheap, fatty beef
  • Decent quality brioche or potato buns
  • American cheese
  • Toppings

Steps

  1. Get cast iron pan super hot.
  2. Loosely ball up your beef.
  3. Smash beef balls into patties onto pan.
  4. Cook for a couple minutes, then flip.
  5. Add cheese and let melt.
  6. Stack patties, then place on bun.
  7. Top with whatever. Enjoy!
