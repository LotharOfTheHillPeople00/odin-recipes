Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Protein of choice
    • Beef
    • Chicken
    • Shrimp
    • Fish
    • Tofu
  • Taco Seasoning (Chili powder and Cumin)
  • Toppings of choice
    • Lettuce
    • Tomato
    • Onion
    • Jalapenos
    • Hot Sauce

Steps

  1. Marinate protein of choice in oil and taco seasoning.
  2. Saute your protein in a pan.
  3. Once cooked, place in tortilla, along with whatever toppings you like. Enjoy!
